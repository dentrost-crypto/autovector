# AutoVector Brand

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete visual identity of AutoVector.

The goal is to maintain one consistent premium brand across:

- Website
- Telegram
- VK
- YouTube
- RuTube
- MAX
- Reality Sandbox
- AMOS Dashboard

---

## Brand Position

AutoVector is a modern technology company helping customers safely purchase vehicles from Korea, China and Japan.

The brand should communicate:

- Trust
- Transparency
- Technology
- Reliability
- Premium Service

---

## Design Principles

Style:

- Premium
- Minimalistic
- Modern
- Technological

Avoid:

- Cheap-looking graphics
- Overloaded layouts
- Random fonts
- Inconsistent colors

---

## Assets

This folder will contain:

- Logos
- Covers
- Social Media Assets
- Watermarks
- Icons
- Brand Guidelines
- Color Palette
- Typography
